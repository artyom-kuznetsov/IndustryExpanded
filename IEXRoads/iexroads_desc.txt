[h2]Load, Ship & Road: IEX Module is an [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2962482328]Industry Expanded Core[/url] optional (but highly recommended) module, adding Road Networks, reworking railways and partially reworking MAPI.[/h2]

[h1]Modular IEX[/h1]
[b]Modular IEX[/b] is former Industry Expanded Mod but split into few separate mods. So you can disable/enable some of them for your preference or compatibility with your current modset.

[h2]We now have Discord! https://discord.gg/3QBTCvjzug[/h2]

[h1]LS&R Module[/h1]
[h2]Contents of this module:[/h2]
- Road Network Building (Limited source of infrastructure before railways)
- 2 New Society Technologies
- New PMs and rebalance for Railways
- Ports give MAPI
- Other Market Access Price Impact Tweaks
- Transportation Good is not local anymore

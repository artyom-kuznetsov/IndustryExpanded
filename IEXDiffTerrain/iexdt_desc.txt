[h2]Difficult Terrain is an [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2962482328]Industry Expanded Core[/url] optional (but highly recommended) module, making most of mountain/desert/forest negative construction effects counterable.[/h2]

[h1]Modular IEX[/h1]
[b]Modular IEX[/b] is former Industry Expanded Mod but split into few separate mods. So you can disable/enable some of them for your preference or compatibility with your current modset.

[h2]We now have Discord! https://discord.gg/3QBTCvjzug[/h2]

[h1]Difficult Terrain Module[/h1]
[h2]Contents of this module:[/h2]
-Difficult Terrain, Very Difficult Terrain, Densely Forested and Deserted Terrain state traits.
-All of these traits can be removed by researching a respective technology, like dynamite, steam donkey or pneumatic tools.

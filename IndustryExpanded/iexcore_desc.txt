A mod focused on goods, building, production methods and variability expansion, improvement and balancing.

[h1]Modular IEX[/h1]
[b]Modular IEX[/b] is former Industry Expanded Mod but split into few separate mods. So you can disable/enable some of them for your preference or compatibility with your current modset.

[h2]We now have Discord! https://discord.gg/3QBTCvjzug[/h2]

[h2]Features of the IEX Series[/h2]
[b]- Road Networks[/b], allowing you to increase infrastructure capacity before inventing railroads.
[b]- Auto-Expansion[/b] reworked to automatically expand buildings when you actually could use them.
[b]- Market Access Price Impact[/b] rework, MAPI now increases steeper, instead of vanilla spikes from techs.
[b]- Difficult Terrain[/b] - most of mountain/desert/forest negative construction effects are now counterable.

[h1]Industry Expanded Core[/h1]
IEX Core is the main part of Industry Expanded which all the modules depend on.
[h2]Contents of the Core:[/h2]
- 4 New Production Technologies
- Oil Industry Expansion
- New Goods: Fuel and Plastics
- Paper Mills Building Rework
- Textile Mills Building Rework
- Electricity and Services goods are not local anymore
- Enhanced pop needs, pops actually shift to electricity for heating, consume more wine and more tweaks
